Ashley Ng is a Toronto-based user experience designer.

An adaptable designer of many disciplines, Ashley’s work frequently finds itself the intersection of form and function; creativity and commerce; tangible and digital media. With her eyes set on the future, she looks towards the bleeding edge of technology with the same excitement and optimism she embeds into her design practice and work ethic.

In her spare time, she sometimes enjoys the oddity of writing about herself in 3rd person, tending to her growing family of desert plants and losing her page as she flips between physical and digital copies while she rekindles her interest for reading.

(Just kidding, I use a Kobo e-reader.)
